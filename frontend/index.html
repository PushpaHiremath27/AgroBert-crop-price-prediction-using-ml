üåæ About AgroBERT

AgroBERT is a cutting-edge Agricultural Market Intelligence Platform that leverages advanced Machine Learning and sentiment analysis to provide real-time crop price predictions, market insights, and recommendations for farmers, traders, and agricultural businesses across India.

Our Mission

To empower agricultural stakeholders with data-driven insights that enable smarter decision-making in an increasingly complex and volatile agricultural market.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Price Predictions: LSTM, Prophet, and ARIMA ensemble models for accurate forecasting
  • Weather Integration: OpenWeatherMap API for climate impact analysis
  • Sentiment Analysis: NLP-powered analysis of agricultural news and market sentiment
  • Regional Heatmaps: Interactive India map showing price variations across states
  • Crop Recommendations: AI-driven suggestions based on soil, climate, and environmental factors
  • Explainable AI (XAI): SHAP-based feature importance for transparent predictions
  • Chatbot Assistant: Multi-language support for quick agricultural queries
  • Custom Reports: Generate downloadable PDF reports of your analysis

Data Sources

  • Agmarknet API: Live market prices from all major APMCs
  • OpenWeatherMap: Real-time and forecasted weather data
  • FAOSTAT: Historical agricultural production statistics
  • News Scrapers: Automated collection of agricultural news

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Flask with REST API, SQLAlchemy ORM
  • ML Models: LSTM, Prophet, ARIMA, BERT
  • Frontend: Interactive HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript with Tailwind CSS
  • Visualization: Chart.js for charts, D3.js for geospatial heatmaps
  • Cloud Ready: Deployable on Heroku, AWS, or Google Cloud

Who We Are

AgroBERT is a collaborative project developed by final-year engineering students pursuing Bachelor of Engineering in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning from Bapuji Institute of Engineering and Technology (BIET), Davanagere, affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU).

Our team of dedicated students combines expertise in machine learning, deep learning, software engineering, data science, and full-stack development to build innovative solutions that address real-world agricultural challenges. This project represents our commitment to leveraging technology for agricultural advancement and food security.
